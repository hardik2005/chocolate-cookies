# INGRIDIENTS FOR CHOCOLATE COOKIES
1. All purpose Flour - ¾ cup
2. Coco powder, unsweetened - ¼ cup
3. Sugar - ½ cup
4. Baking Soda - ¼ tsp
5. Butter, unsalted - ¼ cup, room temperature
6. Milk - 3 tbsp
7. Salt - a fat pinch

Note - If you are using salted butter, then skip the additional salt.
